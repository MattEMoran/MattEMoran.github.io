Capstone Project


Enertiv Capstone Project - Algorithms to Support Commercial Real Estate

For CSCI 4308 - Software Engineering Project, a team of six students including myself are working with Enertiv to develop machine learning algorithms to support sustainability in commercial real estate. Enertiv's mission is to "decarbonize the built environment". Our team is working on two subprojects - one to automate meter readings and another to optimize HVAC system usage. Both subprojects are utilizing variants of neural networks to accomplish these tasks.
