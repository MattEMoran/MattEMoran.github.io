Service




Anonymous Applications

Blueprint Boulder is an organization at CU that focuses on developing pro-bono software solutions for non-profits.

For the 2021-2022 school year, I and a team of other students developed a portal for hosting job, volunteer, and college applications that was originally intended to support the non-profit Impact on Education, an organization that provides financial aid to educators and students in Boulder County. The partnernship unfortunately ended before the project was completed, but the team decided to finish the project and develop it for general purposes. The team used Django to build a back-end capable of storing applications and an NLP Python script to redact information in order to remove potential bias.

Earth Force

Blueprint Boulder is an organization at CU that focuses on developing pro-bono software solutions for non-profits.

For the 2022-2023 school year, myself and a team of other students are developing a portal for the organization Earth Force, which promotes environmental action civics for youth. The portal is intended to provide a space for student teams to upload submissions to civics challenges. The portal is being developed using ReactJS for front-end and Firebase for back-end.
