The Fermi Experiment

This project was for ASTR 2040 "Search for Life in the Universe". The goal was to illustrate key concepts in the class through a creative medium, such as an essay, a podcast, or through the fine arts. I chose to focus to create a printable board game that takes players from the formation of planets to the development of intelligent life. I had some experience working with the vector graphics program Inkscape before the class, and I wanted to take that interest and extend it by designing all of the game's artwork, including the board itself, cards, and player tokens.
